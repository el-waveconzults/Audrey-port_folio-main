THE WHAT

The Akilaah Agent Portal is a digital platform designed to simplify and enhance the management of cooperative organizations and their members. Designed with simplicity and usability in mind, the portal reduces onboarding times, enhances operational efficiency, and fosters trust among agents, cooperatives, and members. It represents a transformative step in digitizing cooperative management, particularly in addressing the unique needs of its users.

THE CHALLENGE

Before the launch of the Agent Portal, the management of cooperatives and their members at Akilaah was fraught with inefficiencies and frustrations. Agents struggled to juggle fragmented systems, manual processes, and disorganized workflows...

  • For Members: Long onboarding times, inconsistent communication, and a lack of clarity created mistrust and a perception of unreliability.
  • For Cooperatives: Managers struggled to maintain accurate records and faced operational bottlenecks.

This disjointed approach hampered Akilaah’s ability to deliver seamless cooperative management.

© 2025 Akilaah Case Study

RESEARCH AND DISCOVERY

Research and Discovery

To better understand and solve the challenges faced, I and my team conducted:

  • Interviews with Agents: Identified pain points in workflows such as onboarding and tracking commissions.
  • Feedback from Cooperative Managers: Revealed the need for accurate member and contribution tracking.
  • Surveys with Members: Highlighted frustrations with delayed registrations and financial transparency.

KEY INSIGHTS

  1. 85% of users preferred mobile access over desktop due to convenience.
  2. Members needed clear navigation, real-time updates, and visual feedback on financial trends.
  3. Younger users preferred modern design, while older users valued simple functionality.
